Pan Fried Chilli Fish

Description

A delicious, tangy Indo-fusion dish, Chilli Fish is everything an appetizer or a side dish should be. It is crunchy, tasty, filling and full of aromatic flavours to have your taste buds tantalizing. Serve as an appetizer or a side dish at your next dinner party and the guests will surely be impressed!

Ingredients

  • 250 g fish(boneless)
  • 1/2 cup maida
  • 1/2 cup corn flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp soya sauce
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • salt
  • oil
  • spring onions
    • For the sauce:
  • 1 tablespoon Ginger(chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon garlic(chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Green Chilli(chopped)
  • 4 tablespoon soya sauce
  • 5 tablespoon tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chilli sauce
  • 1 tablespoon corn flour

Steps

    How to make the chilli fish
  1. Cut fish into finger pieces
  2. Make a batter with corn flour , maida , baking powder, soya sauce , celery etc
  3. Dip fish pieces into the batter and fry in oil till golden brown. Transfer fish into serving plate
    4. Prepare the sauce
  4. Heat oil in the pan
  5. Saute garlic , ginger and green chilli. Add soya sauce, chilli , tomato sauce to it.
  6. Finally add corn flour mixed with water to it and stir well.Once it starts to boil, remove from fire.
  7. At time of serving , pour the sauce on top of fried fish pieces and mix well.
  8. Garnish with choppped spring onions.
